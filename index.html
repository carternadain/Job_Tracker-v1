Mission

Our mission is to provide a user-friendly and efficient job tracking application that helps job seekers keep track of all the jobs they've applied for, streamline their job search process and focus on finding their dream job. Our application offers a variety of features such as inputting job information, updating the status of applications, searching and filtering job applications by different criteria and continuously working to improve and update the application with new features and functionality.

Story

As a web developer trying to break into the industry, I found myself applying to multiple jobs and struggling to keep track of where I had applied, what the status of each application was and the details of each job. I realized that this was a common problem among job seekers and decided to use my skills as a web developer to create a solution. I developed a job tracking application that allows job seekers to input and store information about the jobs they've applied for, update the status of each application, search and filter their job applications by different criteria and stay organized throughout the job search process.
